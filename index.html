What Is Talabro?


Talabro is a fan made Balatro game made completly in the programming language Python using Pygame


Main Game Concept


There is nothing but poker.

Your goal: to play poker with challenges and buffs you can exchange for currency earned.

No people, only you, and the poker table. What more could you ask for?

The blinds are the closest thing you have to human contact, also Oopy Goopy

There is no world.

And remember, always bet on black - Batman
